We have reached a plateau in the volume of AI referral traffic. 

Here is an example from a B2B SaaS website. You can see an "explosion" in AI traffic from last year... because it started from zero. 

But in just one year, we have already arrived at the peak. ChatGPT is leveling off... and every other platform (Perplexity, Gemini, etc) is barely sending any traffic. 

I have observed this same pattern with a dozen more B2B SaaS websites. 

AI search was not designed for traffic generation. It was designed for displaying answers.

This also suggests AI platform user adoption is quickly saturating. 

If your executive team was hoping for "more AI traffic" you can send them this post. 

There ain't gonna be much more. Upward mobility is clearly capped.
