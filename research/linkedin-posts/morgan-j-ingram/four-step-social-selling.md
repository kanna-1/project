Here's my 4-step formula to do social selling with ease. 

(and how I use Common Room to do it)

1. Leverage Subject Matter Experts

Our buyers already engage with content on the feed.  

So the best place to find them is engaging with someone's post.

This is my process within Common Room. 

• Pull relevant posts from any LinkedIn leader 
• Filter based on exact post, activity, and prospect attributes
• Create targeted lists based on your ICP

This gives us a rich pool of engaged and relevant prospects.

2. Send a connection request

I recommend sending a blank note here. 

We have seen this convert higher across multiple industries. 

However, I know you all are wondering...

"How do I scale this?"

Here's how: 

• Action on your curated lists directly from Common Room
• Identify the right buyers to connect with 
• Visit prospect LinkedIn profiles and send connection requests
  
Connecting with the right buyers on social is key. 

3. Network expansion

It doesn't stop with the curated list. 

We can expand the list to find other leaders like those we already found. 

• Set custom criteria to find the perfect additional connections
• Expand your network efficiently and strategically
• Create a halo effect with multiple touchpoints in each account

The goal is that people know you before you reach out. 

4. Engage

I know you all have been waiting to get into the game. 

Now it's time for action. 

• Leverage the insights on prospects and their organizations
• Craft personalized voice notes or video messages based on insights
• Leverage RoomieAI to generate tailored outreach templates instantly

Turn cold outreach into warm, relevant conversations. 

Bonus Step: Follow up on your personalized message 
2-3 days later. 

That's where you will get most of your responses. 

Happy Pipeline Building.
