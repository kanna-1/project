Ethan has more than 3x'd our automated outbound pipeline since he took over the program 6 months ago.
 
Looking back, that was a mistake.
 
Not because he didn't deliver - he crushed it.
 
The mistake was having him go heads-down on only a single channel.
 
We're in one of the most blue-sky, wide-open environments in years. While he was focused on AOB, there were so many additional channels left untouched.
 
I'm definitely guilty of operating in too much of a mindset of "what do I know, and what do I have high confidence is going to work?" You still need some of that, but you have to leave a lot of green space for, "Hey, this is a crazy idea that might work. Let's go do it, and find out."
 
So that's the energy we're channeling on our growth team right now. We're pulling in engineers to work on some growth stuff and branching out into AEO/GEO/SEO and many other channels. And we're already seeing the fruits of Ethan and Rocco's work here.
 
2026 is a year for your growth team to take big new swings and not be held back by optimizing and iterating.
