If your brand isn’t getting traction, stop blaming the algorithm. 

Look at your content strategy:

Are you repurposing insights across LinkedIn, email, and industry blogs, or are you posting once and hoping for the best?

Most brands are posting once and hoping for the best... 

No empowered sales team.
No republishing the hits. 
No cutting up webinars. 
No remixing the pods. 
No retargeting. 

It's a shame. 

But these teams will be the same ones preaching that this channel is dead. 

Don't fall into the trap. 

Create once. Distribute forever.
