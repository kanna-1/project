Last week, one of my coaching clients (VP of Growth) asked for help developing her personal brand.

She realized something important:

In 2025, product-market fit has become fluid. And as a result, job security is fragile. 

A “safe” role today can be eliminated next quarter.

That’s why building a personal brand matters. It’s the one asset you take with you, no matter what happens at your company.

Here’s how we worked through it:

1. Mapped her career direction. Did she want to stay in growth leadership or eventually pivot?

2. Defined her “lane”. What she wanted to be known for in the growth space, balancing authenticity and value.

3. Brainstormed themes + ideas. Content topics that would reinforce that positioning.

4. Picked her channel. She chose LinkedIn (and might experiment with Substack down the road)

5. Shared my content system. Swipe file → draft → edit. 

I was so excited, when a few days later, she sent me a link to her first post.

I’m not a content marketer. I’m a growth coach. 

But this is what growth leadership looks like: investing in yourself.

And if you’re a growth leader who’s been heads-down… maybe it’s time to lift your head up.
